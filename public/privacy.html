Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Suave ("the app") is a dating and conversation assistant app. This policy explains what information is collected, how it's used, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: your email address and password (securely handled by our authentication provider, Supabase).
  • Content you provide: messages you send to the Reply Generator, Dating Coach, and AI Assistant features, including any images or screenshots you upload.
  • Saved items: replies, messages, and images you choose to save within the app.
  • Feedback: any feedback or bug reports you submit through the in-app feedback feature.
  • Usage data: a daily count of how many AI requests your account makes, used only to apply fair usage limits.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the app's core features (generating replies, coaching advice, answering questions, creating images).
  • To let you save and revisit past conversations and generated content.
  • To maintain fair usage limits and prevent abuse of the service.
  • To review feedback and improve the app.

Third-Party Services

The app relies on the following third-party services to function:

  • Supabase — handles account authentication and stores your account data, conversations, saved items, and feedback in a secure database.
  • Groq — processes your messages and images to generate AI responses. Your content is sent to Groq's API for this purpose and is subject to Groq's own privacy practices.
  • Pollinations.ai — used to generate AI images based on prompts you provide, when using the Create Image feature.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your account data, conversations, and saved items remain stored until you delete them yourself within the app, or until you request full account deletion. Account deletion requests are reviewed and processed manually within a few days of submission.

Children's Privacy

Suave is not directed at children and is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18, given its focus on dating and relationship content.

Your Rights

You can view, edit, or delete your saved content directly within the app at any time. You can request full account deletion via the Profile section in the app.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the app evolves. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
